Research question

Can a probabilistic, unsupervised model, identify economically interpretable market states and translate uncertainty about them into diversified allocations across Equity Hedge, Opportunistic, Relative Value and Event Driven strategies?

Estimation

For every displayed month, the scaler and mixture model are re-estimated using only observations available up to that point. Components are assigned economic labels based on each group's average pattern of results: stress prioritises high VIX, wide credit spreads and weak equities; inflation shock prioritises inflation and yield pressure; expansion prioritises equity momentum and industrial production; the residual state is interpreted as recovery.

Important limitation: macroeconomic series here use the latest revised history rather than the original data available at the time. This avoids future market observations but does not completely eliminate revision bias.

Verified data sources

Monthly features are derived from Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED): S&P 500, VIX, 10-year Treasury yield, Baa–10Y credit spread, CPI, industrial production and unemployment.
